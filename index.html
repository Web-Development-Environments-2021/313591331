About Me

Morgan Freeman

Hello, my name is Morgan Freeman and i was born on June 1, 1937.
I was born in Memphis, Tennessee, raised in Mississippi where I began acting in school plays
I'm an American actor, director and narrator.
I appeared in a range of film genres portraying character roles and i'm particularly known for my distinctive deep voice.
I won various accolades, including an Academy Award, a Golden Globe Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award.
Lately, I been doing great commercials for israel company, named Tadiran.
I spent tons of time scraping together money to go to the movies.Some of my earliest matinee idols were actors like:
Gary Cooper, Spencer Tracy and Sidney Poitier, the first African-American to win an Oscar for Best Actor.
I have divorced twice, and i have 4 lovely children:Alfonso, Deena, Morgana and Saifoulaye.
Still, I can testify i'm pretty isolated, and i would love for some company!
